Nissan is a globally renowned and long-established automobile manufacturer. It dates back to the "Kwai Shinsha Motor Car Works" in 1911, and the "Dat" model was launched in 1914. Nissan Motor Co., Ltd. was officially founded in 1933. Since then, it has been developing, introducing foreign technologies to improve product quality, and gradually making its mark in the US market in the 1960s, achieving significant sales growth in the 1970s and becoming one of the world's top ten automakers. In 1999, it formed an alliance with Renault and achieved remarkable development through revitalization plans. Today, Nissan has production bases and sales networks worldwide, offering a wide range of vehicles like sedans, SUVs, pickups, and sports cars, such as Sylphy, X-Trail, and Navara. Known for comfort, reliability, and high cost-effectiveness, it has won the favor of consumers. Technologically innovative, its VG and VQV6 engines were on the "Ward's 10 Best Engines" list for 14 consecutive years, and it's actively exploring the new energy vehicle field. In China, since exporting the first Nissan Cedric sedan in 1973, it has established Zhengzhou Nissan and Dongfeng Nissan Passenger Vehicle Co., Ltd., operating three brands: Nissan, Venucia, and Infiniti, with many popular models.
