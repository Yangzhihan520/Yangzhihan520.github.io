GT-R35



The Nissan GT-R35 is a renowned high-performance sports car. Debuted in 2007, it's powered by a 3.8L V6 twin-turbo engine, delivering strong horsepower and torque. Its dual-clutch transmission ensures quick shifts. With excellent chassis and suspension tuning, it offers superb handling. The exterior is aggressive and aerodynamic, featuring distinctive lines and quad taillights. Inside, it has advanced tech like a multi-functional panel and a good audio system. Safety features are also comprehensive. In a word, the GT-R35 is a perfect mix of power, design and technology.
